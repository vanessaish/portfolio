About Me

Bridging Code & Creativity

I'm a passionate software developer and web designer from the beautiful hills of Rwanda. Currently pursuing Graphic Design & Web Design at Kigali International Art College, I bring a unique perspective that combines technical expertise with artistic sensibility.

My journey began with a fascination for how technology can transform ideas into reality. With a strong foundation in Software Development from Lycée de Nyanza, I've developed skills across multiple programming languages and design tools. My experience as an intern at Improved Hub taught me the value of international collaboration and strengthened my ability to communicate across cultures in English and French.

I believe in creating digital solutions that are not just functional, but beautiful and accessible. Whether it's developing a robust web application or crafting an intuitive user interface, I approach every project with creativity, dedication, and attention to detail.
